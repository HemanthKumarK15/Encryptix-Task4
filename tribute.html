Mr.K.DEMULLU

is a Farmer




He is my father

I'm proud to be his son. He sacrifices everything for me. He farms the land everyday. I want to help him dialy it is one of my best opportunity to help him in his work. And he also drives the tractor. In other fields also he works more. He works a lot. He studied upto 10thclass. But he losts his hallticket while he going to the exam. Otherwise now he will be settled in a well position. That one day changes all his fate. He never regrets in his way. He worked daily and earn money and he looks his parents. In those days only by his hardwork and family members support he bought a tractor and land. He was the elder son of his parents. He took the entire responsibility of his family. If we listen his history we can write one book on his biography. He always teaches his children to walk in a good path. He bought many things for his children. He feed his children everything what they wants. Sometimes I may thought he done his Ph.D in agriculture. He farms very well but due to the market conditions he left half of the farming and mostly he went for ploughing in other fields with tractor and he earns money and he invests his money in his farming. He went for work in other districts and states in those days. He sacrificed his young age for his family responsibilities. And after his marriage he works alot in his agriculture land. And daily he went for the market by walk. He walked daily 30-50 km for the market to sold the vegetables. In those days the villages are not grown up well. So there is no transportation. In case any emergency they ran for ten's kilometres for their needs. He swims very well. He is my teacher in swimming. He teaches very well.
